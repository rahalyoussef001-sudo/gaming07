Neverness to Everness (NTE): The Ultimate Beginner's Guide to Hesperia City

📅 Published: July 2026
🎮 Platform: Windows PC / Console
🔥 Type: Action RPG / Open World

Welcome to Hesperia City, a vibrant neon metropolis where ordinary urban life meets the bizarre supernatural. Developed by Hotta Studio, Neverness to Everness (NTE) is the brand new ARPG sensation combining supernatural action combat, sports car customization, and life simulation.

For players stepping into this universe for the first time, Hesperia City can feel overwhelming. From managing anomaly outbreaks, tuning high-performance cars, to picking the right team composition, this comprehensive guide gathers all essential tips to kickstart your journey.

What is Neverness to Everness? The Premise

In NTE, you take on the role of an Anomalist—a specialized investigator equipped with unique powers tasked with managing "anomalies". Anomalies are reality-warping supernatural events that mutate everyday objects, turning vending machines or traffic lights into monsters and puzzles.

But NTE is far from a standard instanced dungeon crawler. In your downtime, you explore a sprawling city heavily inspired by Akihabara and Shinjuku, purchase real estate, manage businesses, and drive custom sports cars in illegal street racing events.

Combat System: Mastering Elemental Reactions

Combat in NTE is based on a three-character squad that you swap in real-time during fights. Each character has a specific weapon class (swords, firearms, magical anomaly channels) and an elemental attribute.

To defeat high-tier bosses, you must chain elemental reactions together. Key combinations include:

  • Superconduct (Volt + Cryo): Significantly slows down enemies and doubles all physical damage taken.
  • Overload (Pyro + Volt): Triggers a devastating AOE explosion that instantly breaks heavy shield gauges.
  • Crystallize (Geo + Cryo): Grants active shields to your character while creating armor shards on the floor.

💡 Pro Combat Tip

Never start fights with your Main Damage Dealer (DPS). Drop support skills first (which remain active off-field), then switch to your main DPS to benefit from full elemental multipliers!

Urban Driving: Tuning and Races

This is NTE's standout feature: traversal isn't just about parkour or grappling hooks. You have access to a full garage to store custom sports cars inspired by real-world vehicles.

The game features an extremely deep tuning system: body paint, custom wheels, spoilers, underglow neons, and engine performance modifications (turbo, exhaust, suspension). The driving controls are arcade-style, making it easy to drift through tight intersections.

5 Essential Tips for NTE Beginners

  1. Prioritize Story Chapter 1: This unlocks access to your first garage, car tuning systems, and the daily bounty quest board.
  2. Save Your Gacha Currencies: Save your limited-banner gacha tokens for high-tier Shield-Breakers, who are essential for late-game combat content.
  3. Check the Rooftops: Hesperia City's rooftops hide secret anomaly chests containing rare weapon evolution components. Use character grappling hooks to scale buildings.
  4. Upgrade Your First Car: Even your starter car, once upgraded with a Level 2 turbocharger, is enough to win early underground racing tournaments for quick cash.
  5. Check the City Bulletin Board: Secondary investigation requests grant neighborhood reputation points and items to customize your apartment.
