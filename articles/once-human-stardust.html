Once Human: Complete Stardust Farming Guide to Maximize Your Progression

📅 Published: July 2026
🎮 Platform: Windows PC
🔥 Type: Farming / Crafting Guide

In the unforgiving post-apocalyptic world of Once Human, which stands as one of the best PC RPGs 2026, Stardust Source is the most precious and sought-after endgame resource. Without it, you cannot craft legendary weapons, repair high-tier armor sets, or power your advanced electrical generators.

Whether you're looking to dominate PvP servers or clear the toughest PvE dungeons, mastering Stardust farming routes is essential. Here's our complete guide covering the best outdoor farming methods and passive base refinement techniques.

Why Is Stardust So Critical in Once Human?

Stardust Source is integral to nearly every advanced crafting recipe in the game. It's the alien essence that mutated Earth's biosphere. Its primary uses include:

  • Tier V Equipment Crafting: Legendary schematics require Stardust-infused ingots.
  • Energy Refinement: Used to manufacture special ammunition essential for fighting Rift Entity bosses.
  • Tactical Armor Repairs: High-level repairs demand a constant Stardust tax.

Best Farming Zones (Optimal Routes)

Raw Stardust is not found in standard iron or copper mines. To harvest it from exploration, you must target highly contaminated zones:

1. Red Sands Zone

This is the #1 farming spot for Level 40+ players. The abandoned military camps and refineries of Red Sands are filled with sealed crates containing raw Stardust Source. Enemies in this area also drop appreciable quantities of Stardust dust.

2. Global Refinery Events

Scan your world map for active refinery defense events. By defending the refinery against waves of Stardust creatures, you can earn hundreds of Stardust Source units at the end of the countdown. Team up with 4 players to maximize output!

💡 Supernatural Tip

Make sure to carry "Stardust Tea" consumables before heading out to farm. This tea boosts your radiation resistance while increasing Stardust drops from monster kills by 20%!

Base Refinement & Passive Crafting

If you prefer passive home production, Once Human lets you craft Stardust Source directly in your territory using your Memetics tech tree specializations:

  • Territory Stardust Refiner: By unlocking this specialization, you can transform mixed petroleum ore and Stardust dust into refined Stardust Source using power cells.
  • Contaminated Water Specialization: Install water extraction pumps in high-pollution zones (purple areas on the minimap). Filter this contaminated water at your base to extract acidic particles and Stardust residues.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can you buy Stardust with real money?

No, Stardust cannot be purchased with real money. It can only be obtained through gameplay, farming, or player-to-player trading in secure exchange zones.

What level is recommended for farming Red Sands?

We strongly recommend being at least Level 38 with Tier IV weapons to comfortably handle anomalies in this region.

Where should I place my base for optimal passive farming?

The best location is at the border of the Red Sands pollution zone. This gives you access to both petroleum resources and high pollution concentration for your automatic filters.
