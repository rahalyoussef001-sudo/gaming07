S-Tier Characters: Anomaly Cleaners to Pull Immediately

These characters define the meta. They require minimal investment to provide massive value and are highly recommended targets for your banner pity strategy.

Hotori (Volt / Main DPS)

Hotori stands as the absolute queen of area-of-effect damage. Her active skill, Volt Cascade, creates magnetic field zones that pull minor anomalies together while dealing continuous electric tick damage. Swapping to her after deploying supports triggers immediate reactions like Superconduct, shredding enemy resistances. If you are speed-clearing city containment trials, Hotori is indispensable.

Nanami (Cryo / Support-Healer)

Healing in NTE is highly valuable due to the absence of food consumption during active combat. Nanami is the premier healer, scaling her active healing with her maximum Defense stats. Furthermore, her ultimate freezes all non-boss entities for 4 seconds, providing crucial breathing room during chaotic multi-target anomaly breaches.

A-Tier Characters: Powerful Specialists

These characters excel in their specific roles and are key components for high-level elemental reactions.

Miki (Pyro / Shield Breaker)

Elite anomaly bosses possess heavy shields that reduce all incoming damage by 50%. Miki, equipped with heavy gauntlets, has the highest shield-break value in the game. Chaining her Pyro strikes with a Volt applicator triggers the Overload reaction, instantly breaking shield gauges and leaving bosses in a vulnerable state.

Ren (Cryo / Off-Field sub-DPS)

Ren acts as a turret character. When she deploys her Cryo pistols, she shoots frost bullets that remain active even when you swap her out. This makes it incredibly easy to maintain Cryo elements on targets, facilitating continuous reactions when switching to your Main DPS.

Best Team Compositions & Elemental Synergies

Rather than picking characters based solely on tier lists, NTE rewards smart squad composition. Chaining elements in the correct order is the key to unlocking late-game damage.

1. The Superconduct Freeze Team (High Control)

  • Main DPS: Hotori (Volt)
  • Sub-DPS: Ren (Cryo)
  • Healer/Support: Nanami (Cryo)

How to Play: Start by deploying Ren's Cryo pistols to constantly apply Cryo to targets. Swap to Nanami to group targets and freeze them. Finally, bring in Hotori to trigger Superconduct, slow the enemies, and wipe them out.

2. The Melt-Overload Burst Team (High Break)

  • Main DPS: Hotori (Volt)
  • Shield Breaker: Miki (Pyro)
  • Support: Sora (Geo)

How to Play: Use Sora to establish a protective Geo shield. Switch to Miki to apply Pyro and perform heavy strikes against shield gauges. Once the shield breaks, swap to Hotori to trigger Overload bursts and finish the boss.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who is the best character in Neverness to Everness?

Hotori (Volt) is currently the best main DPS character in the game, praised for her immense area-of-effect magnetic attacks and high scaling multipliers.

Is the starter character worth upgrading in NTE?

Yes, the starter Anomalist is a highly versatile A-tier character. They can switch elements between Volt and Geo, providing crucial flexibility for early-to-mid game progression.

How many characters do I need for a complete team?

A standard NTE squad consists of three active characters that you can swap in real-time to trigger elemental reactions.

Conclusion

While Hotori and Nanami are the clear winners of our 2026 character rankings, remember that elemental synergies are the true driving force in NTE's combat. Focus on pairing complementary elements (Volt and Cryo/Pyro) to trigger high-damage reactions.
