The shift from traditional mechanical switches (Cherry MX, Gateron traditional linear) to magnetic Hall Effect (HE) switches has revolutionized keyboard latency. In fast-paced survival combat or action RPG encounters like Once Human Stardust Battles, instant key resetting eliminates input delay.

How Rapid Trigger Works

Unlike standard mechanical switches that require the stem to cross a fixed physical reset height (usually 2.0mm), magnetic HE switches measure exact stem location via magnetic flux. The moment your finger lifts up by even 0.1mm, the key de-actuates, allowing immediate re-press or direction shifts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Hall Effect keyboards suitable for daily typing?

Yes. Because magnetic actuation points are fully customizable via software, users can set deeper actuation (2.5mm) for typing to avoid typos, and shallow actuation (0.2mm) for gaming profiles.
