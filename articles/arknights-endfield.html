The Hook: Conquer and Automate Talos-II

In Arknights: Endfield, players take the role of the Endfield Administrator. You are deployed to Talos-II, a dangerous planet plagued by "The Blight"—a mysterious crystalline decay that mutates local wildlife and warps environments. Humans live in isolated dome cities, struggling for survival.

Your job isn't just to fight; it's to construct infrastructure. You must build an automated industrial network across the planet to extract resources, purify contaminated sectors, and connect outpost hubs back to the central base.

The Core System: Conveyor Belts & Processing Plants

The standout feature of Endfield is the Automated Industry Complex (AIC). Rather than manually mining copper or herbs, you build automated machinery:

  • Logistics Network: Place automatic drills on resource nodes, connect them using conveyor belts to smelters, and route processed bars directly into your automated assembly lines.
  • Power Grid: Deploy solar panels, wind turbines, and battery arrays. Run electrical poles across valleys to power your remote factories.
  • Defensive turrets: The Blight's monsters will attack your outposts. Set up automated defenses to protect your production loops while you explore.

Base Building Tip

Automate your ammo and potion production early! Linking your chemical refiner directly to your character upgrade terminal means you will automatically obtain high-tier combat buffs while out exploring ruins.

Combat: Real-Time Tactical Swapping

Combat is fast and fluid. You lead a squad of four Operators, controlling one while the other three fight using advanced AI. You can swap between characters instantly, chaining special abilities to stagger bosses. Combining physical attacks with elemental energy triggers massive blasts that clear groups of enemies quickly.
